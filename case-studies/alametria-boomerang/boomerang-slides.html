Drop + Return +
Escape
state.DROP
The Gap
31+ consecutive days with no purchase or trading activity. Not gone — just absent. Recovery window opens.
trigger: 31d_silence
state.RETURN
The Boomerang
Customer re-engages after a Drop. Makes a purchase or resumes trading. A new cycle opens with its own data.
event: boomerang ↺
state.ESCAPE
True Churn
Extended absence with no return signal. Tracked separately — avoids wasted campaign spend on those truly gone.
signal: no_re-engagement
[INFO] 5-cycle tracking active. Each Drop→Return journey stores LTD bucket, revenue, withdrawals, purchase count.
