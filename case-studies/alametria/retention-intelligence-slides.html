Discovery #1

Stacking Isn't a Bug.
It's a Feature.

83%

of new traders buy multiple accounts in their first week

It's a deliberate strategy — run multiple challenges simultaneously to maximize the odds of passing. Once we understood this, we stopped treating stackers as anomalies and started building for them.

Days 1–3
Peak buying. Welcome them with bundle messaging — "Maximize Your Chances."
Days 4–7
Evaluating early results. Send tips: "Managing multiple accounts effectively."
Day 7+
Loyalty hook: "Your next account is 10% off." Lock in the relationship early.
